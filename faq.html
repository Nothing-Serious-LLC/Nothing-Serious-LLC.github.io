Frequently Asked Questions

Is Quests free?

Yes. Core functionality is free. We may add an optional premium tier for advanced analytics and exclusive challenges.

Which platforms are supported?

iOS 14 + and Android 9 +. A web dashboard is on our roadmap.

How do I invite friends?

Open any quest → tap “Invite” → share the link or SMS code.

Can I create private quests?

Absolutely. Toggle “Private” when creating a quest and only invited users will see it.

How do I delete my data?

In the app: Profile → Settings → Delete Account. All personal data is erased within 24 hours.
