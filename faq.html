Frequently Asked Questions

Is Quests free?

Yes – core features are free. We may introduce a premium tier for advanced analytics and exclusive challenges.

Which platforms are supported?

iOS (14 +) and Android (9 +). A web dashboard is on our roadmap.

How do I invite friends?

Open a quest → tap “Invite” → share the unique link or SMS code.

Can I create private quests?

Yes. When you create a quest, toggle “Private” – only invited users see it.

How do I delete my data?

Inside the app: Profile → Settings → Delete Account. All personal data is erased within 24 h.
