Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Nothing Serious LLC (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates the Quests mobile application and nothingserious.info (collectively, the “Service”). This page explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide – name, email address, avatar photo, quest check-ins.

Automatically collected – device type, OS version, app interactions, IP address (via analytics).

SMS data – if you opt-in to SMS invites, your phone number is processed by Twilio.

2. How We Use Information

• Create and manage your account
• Sync progress across devices
• Respond to support requests
• Improve and debug the Service
• Send optional notifications and product updates

3. Legal Bases (GDPR)

We process your data under (a) performance of a contract, and (b) legitimate interests to improve our product. Where required, we request consent (e.g., marketing emails).

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We never sell personal data. We share it only with processors necessary to run the Service (e.g., Supabase hosting, Twilio SMS). All vendors are bound by confidentiality and GDPR-compliant DPAs.

5. Data Retention

We retain data while your account is active. You can delete your account at any time in Settings; data is purged within 24 hours.

6. Security

Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. Internal access is limited to employees who need it.

7. Children’s Privacy

Quests is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect their data.

8. Your Rights

You may access, correct, or delete your information. Email to exercise these rights.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. We’ll notify you via the app or email.

10. Contact

If you have questions, email or write to Nothing Serious LLC, 44th St, New York, NY 10017, USA.
